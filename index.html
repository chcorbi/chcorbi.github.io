    I am a PhD Student at CEDRIC (CNAM) and valeo.ai, under the supervision of Nicolas Thome and Patrick Pérez. My research interests include uncertainty estimation, robustness, heterogeneous and multimodal data analysis in computer vision and autonomous driving applications. Previously, I worked as a Computer Vision Engineer at Earthcube and beforehand as a Research Intern at Heuritech. I graduated from Master 2 Data Sciences at Université Paris-Scalay in 2017 and I obtained a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Ecole Centrale de Lille in 2016.
